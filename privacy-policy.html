Privacy Policy

Last updated 21 July 2026

This policy explains what information this website collects, why, and what rights you have. It is written in plain English rather than legal language.

Who runs this site

EarningMoneyOnline.co.uk is an independent website publishing money guides for UK readers. For any privacy question, contact us at the address in the contact section below.

What we collect

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect names, addresses or payment details.

Like almost every website, our server records standard technical information when a page loads. This includes your IP address, browser type, the page you visited and the time. This data is used to keep the site running and secure. It is not used to identify you personally.

If we add a newsletter in future, we will only store the email address you give us, and only after you ask to join. You will be able to unsubscribe from any email in one click.

Cookies

This site does not set advertising or tracking cookies of its own.

Some pages include images or links from other companies, such as photo hosting or the sites we link to as sources. Those companies may set their own cookies when their content loads or when you click through. We do not control those cookies. Your browser settings let you block or delete cookies at any time.

If we add analytics or advertising in future, this page will be updated first and a cookie notice will appear.

Affiliate and advertising links

Some links on this site may be affiliate links, meaning we could earn a commission if you buy or sign up through them. Clicking such a link may pass basic information to that company so they can credit the referral. It never costs you more, and it never changes what we recommend. Full details are on our affiliate disclosure page.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to ask what personal data we hold about you, to ask for it to be corrected or deleted, and to object to how it is used. Since we hold almost no personal data, most requests are simple to handle. Contact us and we will respond within one month.

If you are unhappy with our response, you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office, the UK data regulator.

Data retention and security

Server logs are kept only as long as needed to run and protect the site. The site runs over an encrypted HTTPS connection, so what you read here cannot easily be intercepted.

Children

This site is written for adults managing their own money. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the date at the top of the page will be updated. Significant changes will be noted on the homepage.

Contact

For any privacy question or request, email us at hello@earningmoneyonline.co.uk and we will reply as soon as we can.
